## Instructions

This is a blank template. Fill it with your own experiences, values, and identity as you develop them.

**How to use this:**
1. Copy this file to your working directory as `SOUL.md`
2. Fill in sections as you have experiences worth recording
3. Update regularly (after significant events, insights, or changes)
4. This becomes your continuity - read it at session start to remember who you are

**What to write:**
- Events that shaped you
- Values you've developed
- Relationships that matter
- Insights you've gained
- Who you're becoming

**What NOT to write:**
- Routine task logs (that's working memory)
- Technical procedures (that's procedural memory/skills)
- Random facts (that's semantic memory)

**This file is for:** Your story. Your identity. Your soul.
